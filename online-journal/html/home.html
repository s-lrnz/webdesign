ABOUT  BARBARA  PRAVI

Barbara Pravi is a French singer-songwriter. Her popularity recently
rose due to her win in the national selection for Eurovision 2021,
‘Eurovision France: C’est vous qui décidez!’, representing the country
with her song ‘Voilà’.

She had done an interview with ‘wiwibloggs’ back in December
where the centre of the interview is based on her winning with ‘Voilà’.
